CHEM 356 Notes

Basic mathematics

  • Energy of a photon: $E = h\nu = h\frac{c}{\lambda} = hc\tilde{\nu}_{[m]}$
  • Black body radiation: $\lambda_{max} \cdot T = \frac{hc}{4.965 k_B}$
  • De Broglie wavelength: $\lambda = \frac{h}{p}$

Bra|c|ket notation

  • $\lt \Psi | \Psi \gt = \int^{\infty}_{-\infty} \Psi^{*} \Psi d\tau$
  • $\lt \Psi | \hat{A} | \Psi \gt = \int^{\infty}_{-\infty} \Psi^{*} \hat{A} \Psi d\tau = \int^{\infty}_{-\infty} \Psi^{*} a \Psi d\tau = a \int^{\infty}_{-\infty} \Psi^{*} \Psi d\tau$

Expectation, Variance, and Standard Error

  • Expectation: $\lt x \gt = \int^{\infty}_{-\infty} x \Psi^{*} \Psi dx$
  • Something: $\lt x^2 \gt = \int^{\infty}_{-\infty} x^2 \Psi^{*} \Psi dx$
  • Variance: $\sigma^2 = \lt x^2 \gt - (\lt x \gt)^2$
  • Standard Error: $\sigma = \sqrt{\lt x^2 \gt - (\lt x \gt)^2}$
