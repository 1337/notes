Pyruvate dehydrogenase complex (PDC)

  • Three subunits: Pyruvate decarboxylase (E1), Dihydrolipoyl transacetylase (E2), Dihydrolipoyl dehydrogenase (E3)
  • 2 substrate cofactors: NAD+ and CoASH
  • 3 bound cofactors: TPP, lipoic acid, FAD

PDC carries out 5 reactions:

  • E1: the pyruvate dehydrogenase subunit (Reactions 1,2)
  • Lipoic acid acts as a flexible arm
  • E2: dihydrolipoamide transacetylase (Reaction 3)
  • E3: lipoamide dehydrogenase (Reaction 4,5)
