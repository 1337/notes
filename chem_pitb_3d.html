Particle in a Box: Time-independent, 3D version

The 3D schrodinger equation is a simple extension to the 1D version. Now we have new bounds:

$0 \leq x \leq a, 0 \leq y \leq b, 0 \leq z \leq c$

So now we solve the equation

$\begin{eqnarray*} \hat{H} \Psi &=& E \Psi \\ \frac{\hbar^2}{2m} \nabla^2 \Psi (x,y,z) &=& E \Psi (x,y,z) \\ \end{eqnarray*}$

The three dimensions are apparently independent.
So, split $\Psi (x,y,z)$ into $\Psi (x,y,z) = \Psi_x (x) \cdot \Psi_y (y) \cdot \Psi_z (z)$.

$\begin{eqnarray*} \frac{\hbar^2}{2m} \nabla^2 \Psi (x) - E \Psi (x) &=& 0 \\ (\Psi_x + \Psi_y + \Psi_z) + \frac{2mE}{\hbar^2} \Psi_x\Psi_y\Psi_z &=& 0 \\ \end{eqnarray*}$

You can now split this equation into three near-identical, 1D ones. See derivation)

$\begin{eqnarray*} \Psi_x (x) &=& \sqrt{\frac{2}{a}} \sin{\frac{n_x \pi}{a}x} \\ \Psi_y (y) &=& \sqrt{\frac{2}{b}} \sin{\frac{n_y \pi}{b}y} \\ \Psi_z (z) &=& \sqrt{\frac{2}{c}} \sin{\frac{n_z \pi}{c}z} \\ \end{eqnarray*}$

Re-realise that $\Psi(x,y,z) = \Psi_x \cdot \Psi_y \cdot \Psi_z$:

$\begin{eqnarray*} \Psi(x,y,z) &=& \Psi_x \cdot \Psi_y \cdot \Psi_z \\ &=& \sqrt{\frac{2}{a}} \sin{\frac{n_x \pi}{a}x} \sqrt{\frac{2}{b}} \sin{\frac{n_y \pi}{b}y} \sqrt{\frac{2}{c}} \sin{\frac{n_z \pi}{c}z} \\ &=& \sqrt{\frac{8}{abc}} \sin{\frac{n_x \pi}{a}x} \sin{\frac{n_y \pi}{b}y} \sin{\frac{n_z \pi}{c}z} \\ \end{eqnarray*}$

($= (\frac{2}{L^2})^{\frac{3}{2}} \sin{\frac{n_x \pi x}{L}} \sin{\frac{n_y \pi y}{L}} \sin{\frac{n_z \pi z}{L}}$ if the box is a cube.)

And the energy is now:

$\begin{eqnarray*} E_{xyz} &=& E_x + E_y + E_z \\ &=& \frac{n_x^2 h^2}{8ma} + \frac{n_y^2 h^2}{8mb} + \frac{n_z^2 h^2}{8mc}\\ &=& \frac{h^2}{8m}(\frac{n_x^2}{a^2} + \frac{n_y^2}{b^2} + \frac{n_z^2}{c^2})\\ \end{eqnarray*}$

Degeneracy

If any of the three quantum numbers are the same, then we get degeneracy, e.g. $[2,1,1]$, $[1,2,1]$, and $[1,1,2]$.
